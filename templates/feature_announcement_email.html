We’ve been busy — here’s what’s new in Auto-Cart this week.

⭐ Pantry Staples

Mark any pantry list item as a staple — things like olive oil, garlic, or butter that you always have on hand. Staple items stay visible on your list but appear dimmed so you know you don’t need to buy them. Tap the star icon next to any ingredient to toggle it.

🏷️ Recipe Tags

Organize your recipe box with tags like quick, vegetarian, or weeknight. Open any recipe to add tags, then use the filter bar above your recipe list to instantly narrow down what’s shown. Great for when the recipe box starts to grow.

🔀 Recipe Sharing Between Households

Belong to more than one household? Open any recipe and use the Export to Other Households section to copy it across — handy for sharing a family favourite from your home household to your vacation house or a roommate’s group.

🔤 A–Z Pantry Sort

Use the new sort buttons in the pantry list header to switch between default order and alphabetical. No page reload — the list re-sorts instantly.
